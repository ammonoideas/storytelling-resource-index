VINTAGE DANCER

Vintage Dancer is a website dedicated to historical fashion from the Victorian era through the 1990s. The site offers a general overview of the various styles of fashion from each decade, sorted by gender, by occasion (e.g. daywear, eveningwear), and by article of clothing (e.g. shirts, pants, dresses, sleepwear).
