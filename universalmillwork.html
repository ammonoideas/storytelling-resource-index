UNIVERSAL MILLWORK DESIGN BOOK NO. 20

If you're looking for references for 1920s interior design, Universal Millwork has you covered. The book is a catalogue of different styles of doors, windows, blinds, etc. from 1920, which is useful for drawing and designing enironments from that time period.

The contents of this book may not be front-and-center features of most artworks, but taking into account common design elements of the historical era from which you're drawing can really tie a space together. Sometimes it's the little details that make the biggest difference.
