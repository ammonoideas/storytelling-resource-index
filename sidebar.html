Welcome to EX LIBRIS AMMONOIDEAS, my personal library for storytelling resources. Here I have collected all of the links I normally use in the process of character creation, writing, worldbuilding, and visual development in the hopes that others might find this useful as well.

My main interests lie in the realms of fantasy, history (1890s-1940s mainly), and the aquatic, so the range of resources currently present in this index biases toward those subjects. The list will hopefully continue to grow as I expand my creative horizons in the future.
