THE HISTORY OF NANTUCKET

The History of Nantucket was written by Obed Macy and published in the year 1835. It is an account of, you guessed it, the history of the Massachusetts island of Nantucket, and a valuable secondary source for learning about the subject.

Nantucket is historically known for its involvement in the history of American whaling, so much of the book's contents are dedicated to the subject. Macy covers a variety of topics related to Nantucket's whaling history, ranging from statistics about whaling life to the many industries on land that relied on whaling.
