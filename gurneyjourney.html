GURNEY JOURNEY

James Gurney is a prolific illustrator known for being the author of the books Dinotopia and Color and Light. Gurney Journey, in turn, is his personal blog, where he's collected and created all kinds of resources for illustration. These resources cover a wide range of subjects, from tutorials to techniques to history to references.

A personal highlight for me on the website is his blog post about J.C. Leyendecker's painting process.
