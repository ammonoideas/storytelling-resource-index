THE PHRONTISTERY

The Phrontistery is a vast collection of all sorts of obscure and unusual words. The site contains glossaries on a variety of esoteric topics, such as "Carriages and Chariots" or "Units of Measurement". You can browse its Compendium of Lost Words or the Loquacious Location of Lipograms for endless word-related fun.
